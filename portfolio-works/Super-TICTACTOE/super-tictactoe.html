Super Tic-Tac-Toe

Super Tic-Tac-Toe is an exciting enhancement of the classic Tic-Tac-Toe game, built with Python and Pygame. Challenge a friend or a smart AI across adjustable difficulty levels, all within a smooth interface complete with engaging sound effects.

Explore the Project

Source code available on GitHub: Source on GitHub

Features

  • - Single and Multiplayer Modes: Play against a friend or challenge an AI opponent.

  • - AI Difficulty Settings: Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard to suit your skill level.

  • - Interactive UI: Real-time feedback and smooth animations during play.

  • - Sound Toggle: Enable or disable background music and effects.

Gameplay

The game is played on a 3×3 grid of smaller 3×3 grids (a super-grid). Players alternate turns placing X or O. Win a small grid by lining up three symbols, then aim to win three small grids in a row across the larger super-grid—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

How to Play

  1. Select your game mode from the main menu.
  2. If versus AI, pick your difficulty level.
  3. Click a highlighted small grid to place your symbol (X or O).
  4. Win a small grid by lining up three symbols.
  5. Aim to win three small grids in a row on the super-grid.
  6. After the match, choose Restart or Main Menu.
