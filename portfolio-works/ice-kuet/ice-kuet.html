Ice covered KUET Campus

A unique visual documentation of KUET's campus transformed into a winter wonderland. The project captures snow-and-ice-covered academic buildings, walkways, and transport using high-resolution AI photography and a cinematic video. A rare phenomenon in a tropical region, this collection preserves the fleeting beauty and atmosphere of the storm.
